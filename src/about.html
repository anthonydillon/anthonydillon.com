About me

Anthony Dillon is a Director of Engineering at Canonical. He leads the Web engineering teams, where he helps a rapidly-growing organisation scale its architecture and engineering practices. He is passionate about open source and excels in developing solid design systems to enable rapid, sustainable delivery through decentralised decision-making with a focus on results. When not working, Anthony enjoys running, playing football and spending time with his family.
